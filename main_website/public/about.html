    The goal:

    Brandon sandersons cytonic universe is awesome and I've always wanted to make a video game. Put the two things together and I've always thought you could get a great combo. I also like the hit indie game FTL, since I had never made a game before I thought FTL's formular could be adapted without too much of a learning curve to the cytonic setting.

    I should note that I dont own brandon sandersons works, nor have I gotten any kind of permission to make content for him or his company. This is a fan project, and since I wont be amking any money on it I think it will be ok If I am mistaken I will pull the project down.
